Cycle Syncing Your Workouts

Match your exercise to your cycle phases for better results:

  • Follicular: Try high-intensity workouts when energy rises
  • Ovulation: Peak performance time for challenging workouts
  • Luteal: Switch to moderate strength training
  • Menstrual: Gentle yoga and walking

  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Nature walks
  • Journaling before bed
